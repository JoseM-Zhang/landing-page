Kessoku Band from anime series "Bocchi The Rock!" brings a full-length album including insert song(s), newly recorded song(s) and more for 14 tracks in total. Comes with a Blu-ray featuring creditless intro and outro. Comes with a sticker featuring backstage pass design.

Meet the band members!

Ikuyo Kita

A bright and popular first year of high school. She is in charge of the guitar and vocals of Kessoku Band. She is a cheerful character who likes to interact with people and can talk to her with a smile without hesitation, even when she meets her for the first time. She looks up to Ryo, and her feelings can go too far. She often takes photos and uploads to Isosta.

Ryo Yamada

A cool and solitary second-year high school student. She is the bassist of Kessoku Band and Nijika's best friend. Her hobbies are out of the ordinary, and she rejoices when called a weirdo. She lives with a wealthy family but never has any money because she spends it all on instruments.

Hitori Gotoh

An extremely timid and introverted first-year student in high school. She is in charge of the lead guitar of Kessoku Band. She dreams of playing in a band that might let even an introverted girl shine and starts playing the guitar. Although her skill is the real deal, she can't show it well in a band or public.

Nijika Ijichi

An energetic and cheerful second-year high school student. She is the drummer of Kessoku Band. The organizer of her band who takes care of Hitori was already joined Kessoku Band. She has an older sister, Seika, who is the manager of the live house "STARRY" and has a special feeling for the live house.

So yeah, Kessoku Band’s self-titled debut is a great time. The vocals are excellent, the production is mostly solid, there’s a decent amount of variety, and the highlights are truly quite high. I really loved “Bocchi The Rock”, and I very much hope that future seasons of the anime also have accompanying albums. That would be amazing!

-Fan #1
